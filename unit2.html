Unit II:

Genetics: The Study of Inherited Traits

Overview

This unit enriches your understanding on information passed on from parental generation to their offspring. Some of these traits may be a mutation or alteration from the previous generation. This also includes the discussion on the most important genetic material known as DNA and certain processes involved in genetic replication and production of protein. The different processes are governed by the study of Gregor Johann Mendel who developed the different Laws of Genetics.
