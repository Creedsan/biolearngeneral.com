Introduction to Genetics

The transmission of hereditary materials is the central topic in genetics.The concept of modern genetics has been the focus of studies since the mid-1900s beginning with the work of Gregor Mendel. As technology became more advanced, more work has shed light into the process of inheritance and the view of the central dogma, that is, the process of DNA replication and protein synthesis. Modern technologies developed from this knowledge led to its applications in genetic engineering.
