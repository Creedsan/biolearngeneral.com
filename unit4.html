Unit IV:

Energy Transformation

Overview

This unit focuses on respiration and photosynthetic process as reactions that complements each other to enable life to survive. It will enhance your understanding of major features and events involved such as significant steps in Calvin cycle, glycolysis, and Krebs cycle. At the end of this unit, you will be able to have a deeper understanding on the importance of photosynthesis and cellular respiration to all forms of living things.
